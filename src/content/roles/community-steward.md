## When This Role Is Needed

When the community grows, onboarding needs structure, or discussions need facilitation.

## Typical Requests

- Help a newcomer find their first task
- Improve onboarding documentation
- Connect contributors across squads

## How To Start

Welcome new members in Telegram or GitHub and ask where they want to contribute.
