## When This Role Is Needed

When a squad needs clarity on problems, priorities, or what to build next.

## Typical Requests

- Define MVP scope
- Prioritize backlog items
- Synthesize interview insights

## How To Start

Pick a project in discovery or planning and offer to help shape the next milestone.
