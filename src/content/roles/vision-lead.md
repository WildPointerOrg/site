## When This Role Is Needed

When a squad or the community needs clarity on purpose, principles, or strategic direction.

## Typical Requests

- Clarify whether an initiative fits WildPointer's mission
- Resolve conflicts between speed and openness
- Align new projects with community values

## How To Start

Read the manifesto, join community discussions, and share how your perspective can strengthen direction.
