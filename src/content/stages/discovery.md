## When to Join This Stage

Join this stage if you like research, interviews, analysis, product thinking, or early experiments.

## What Good Work Looks Like

Good work at this stage is transparent, evidence-based, and focused on real user problems.

## Typical Questions

- Who has this problem?
- How painful is it?
- What solutions already exist?
- Why should we build this?
